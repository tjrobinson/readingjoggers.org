Club Kit

Club colours/kit must be worn when you compete at:
  • Any of the races forming our Club Championship
  • At any event where we compete as a team.

Most races organised under England Athletics (EA) rules include a discount for EA afflilated club. Therefore we strongly encourage members to wear club colours/kit during races.

Wearing RJ colours allows you to spot other RJ membes and also helps build a good club spirit. Its also acts as great advertising for prospective new members.


Please fill in the online order form if you wish to place an order.

  • Its recommend you try your size before you place your order. Order once placed cannot be exchanged for different size.
  • Kit is ordered when we have enough items to place an order with our supplier. Please keep an eye out on facebook for advance warning when orders are going to be placed.
