ABOUT US

Welcome to Reading Joggers.

Reading Joggers is a small and friendly running club, formed in 1977 during the early years of the first jogging boom. We have about 150 active members ranging in age from 18 to 80. Just come along to the Beansheaf Community Centre, Charrington Road, Calcot, Reading RG31 7AW on a Tuesday or Thursday evening at 7:00 PM where a warm welcome awaits you. We aim to leave for our runs about 7:20 PM.

Running keeps you fit, but there's no pressure from us to improve! It's sheer enjoyment that keeps us going! However we have summer and winter handicaps where we try ourselves out over a regular course and a consistency league for those who do wish to improve and for those that do we have a qualified UKA coach who runs coaching sessions and can offer guidance.

The club is affiliated to England Athletics, so your annual subscription (currently £25 of which £14 goes to England Athletics Registration) offers reduced cost of entry to many running events.

If you like running with us then please complete the online club membership form

If you'd like to know more just email your query to the club.

Activities

  • Club social run every Tue at 7:20 pm.
  • Training Session on Thur at 7:20 pm. (see events page)
  • Social runs arranged on most weekends.
  • Consistency League which runs from April - March.
  • Summer & Winter Handicap competition.
  • Participate in local running events.
